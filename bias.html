My Bias – Kim EZ

Kim EZ became my bias because of her powerful stage presence, confidence, and the unique energy she brought to Baby V.O.X.

She stood out not only for her visuals, but for her attitude and charisma, especially during the group’s peak years. Her performances always felt bold, expressive, and unforgettable.

Learning more about her story, personality, and role in Baby V.O.X only strengthened my appreciation for her. Kim EZ represents the strength and individuality that made Baby V.O.X such an iconic first-generation group.

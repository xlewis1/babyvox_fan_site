My Bias – Kan Mi Youn

Kan Mi Youn holds a very special place for me as my second bias in Baby V.O.X. Her charm, elegance, and warmth always stood out, both on stage and off.

I admire how she balanced strong performances with a gentle and genuine personality. Her voice, expressions, and presence gave Baby V.O.X a softness that perfectly complemented the group’s powerful image.

Watching her growth over the years — from her Baby V.O.X days to her solo career — has only deepened my respect and appreciation for her. Kan Mi Youn represents grace, longevity, and heart, and that’s why she remains such an important bias to me.

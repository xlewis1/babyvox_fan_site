Baby V.O.X History: 2003–2004

The final years before disbandment, including albums and member departures.

  • 2003: Album Devotion released, featuring the hit "What Should I Do" and a club mix version of "Coincidence".
  • 2004: Album Ride West released, featuring "Xcstasy" and "Play Remix". The album brings international attention but sparks controversy.
  • October 2004: Shim Eun Jin leaves the group, marking the start of the end for Baby V.O.X's original lineup.
  • April 2005: Yoon Eun Hye departs, leaving only the remaining members for final activities.

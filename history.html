Baby V.O.X History: 1997–1999

Relive the early years of Baby V.O.X, including debuts and lineup changes!

  • July 3, 1997: Baby V.O.X debuts with their first studio album Equalizeher and the single "Hair Cut".
  • 1998: Cha Yumi leaves due to injury; replaced by Kan Mi Youn. Shim Eun Jin and Lee Gai later join for the album Baby Vox II.
  • 1999: Lee Gai leaves the group and is replaced by a 15-year-old Yoon Eun Hye. The single "Get Up" becomes a major hit.

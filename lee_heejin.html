Lee Hee Jin

*** FAN SHRINE CORNER * STATUS: SUB VOCALIST, VISUAL & TALENTED ACTRESS * WELCOME TO THE FAN ARCHIVE! ***

Basic Information

  • Full Name: Lee Hee Jin
  • Born: May 16, 1979
  • Role in Baby V.O.X: Sub Vocalist, Visual
  • Years Active in Group: 1997–2006

Career with Baby V.O.X

Lee Hee Jin was one of the final five members of Baby V.O.X and became known for her elegant visuals and calm stage presence. She participated in the group’s most successful era, contributing to albums and promotions that helped establish Baby V.O.X as one of the defining girl groups of first-generation K-pop.

During her time in the group, she was often recognized as a visual member and appeared in various broadcasts and performances alongside the group’s peak popularity.

Acting Career

After Baby V.O.X, Lee Hee Jin transitioned into acting. She appeared in Korean television dramas as well as stage productions, building a steady acting career that showcased a different side of her talents beyond music.

Her work includes both screen and theater acting, earning her respect as a versatile entertainer rather than remaining limited to idol activities.

Café Owner

In addition to her work as a singer and actress, Lee Hee Jin has also become a café owner. She has been involved in operating a café, including a brunch-style café, marking her move into business outside the entertainment industry.

The café reflects her warm personality and quieter post-idol lifestyle, and it has become a meaningful part of her life after stepping away from full-time entertainment activities.

Legacy

Lee Hee Jin is remembered by fans as a graceful and consistent presence within Baby V.O.X. Her ability to successfully transition into acting and business has made her an example of longevity beyond idol fame.

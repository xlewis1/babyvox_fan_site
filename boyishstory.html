Genre: K‑Pop / R&B / Dance‑Pop / Ballad

Bio / Fun Facts:
Boyish Story is the fifth Korean‑language studio album by Baby V.O.X, released in 2001 during the group’s evolution toward more self‑produced work. All five members participated creatively on this release, with contributions to lyrics and composition to diversify the group’s sound.

The title track “Game Over” embraced a more energetic and “boyish” aesthetic and showed the group breaking from the traditional sexy concept toward a confident style.

Though not as commercially dominant as some earlier releases, Boyish Story remains a fan‑loved and creatively ambitious part of Baby V.O.X’s discography.

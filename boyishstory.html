Boyish Story

📅 Release Date: June 4, 2001

🎤 Members: Kim E‑Z, Shim Eun‑Jin, Kan Mi‑Youn, Yoon Eun‑Hye, Lee Hee‑Jin

🎸 Genre: K‑Pop / R&B / Dance‑Pop / Ballad

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Boyish Story is the fifth Korean‑language studio album by Baby V.O.X, landing in 2001 as the group pivoted toward deeper creative independence. All five members asserted greater control over their musical output during this project, contributing directly to lyricism and melodic compositions to expand and diversify their trademark sound.

The hard-hitting title track “Game Over” completely discarded the conventional delicate pop concepts of the time, confidently embracing an energetic, structural "boyish" look. Though navigating a highly competitive market window, this ambitious alternative record remains an immensely loved classic in first-generation K-pop history.

Shim Eun Jin

*** FAN SHRINE CORNER * STATUS: LEAD DANCER, VOCALIST & FINE ARTIST * WELCOME TO THE FAN ARCHIVE! ***

Shim Eun Jin is a South Korean singer, dancer, and artist best known as a core member of Baby V.O.X. She joined the group during its early years and became known for her strong dance skills, calm stage presence, and versatility.

  • Born: February 6, 1981
  • Position: Lead Dancer, Vocalist
  • Baby V.O.X Tenure: 1998–2005
  • Notable Era: Played a major role during the group’s peak popularity in the late 1990s and early 2000s

Solo Career (Zeeny)

After leaving Baby V.O.X in 2005, Shim Eun Jin briefly pursued a solo career under the stage name Zeeny. Her solo work showcased a more mature and experimental image compared to her group activities, marking a transitional phase in her career.

  • Stage Name: Zeeny
  • Solo Activity: 2005
  • Concept: Mature pop with artistic visuals

Later Career

Following her time in music, Shim Eun Jin shifted her focus toward visual art and painting. She has since become known as a professional artist, holding exhibitions and earning respect in the Korean art scene.

  • Profession: Visual artist / painter
  • Known For: Abstract and emotional artwork
  • Legacy: A rare example of an idol successfully transitioning into the fine arts world

Kim EZ (Kim Hee-Jung)

Kim EZ was the main rapper and leader of Baby V.O.X. She was part of the group from its early days in 1997 until 2006, helping to shape the group's identity and success.

  • Born: January 31, 1979
  • Role: Main Rapper, Leader
  • Years Active in Baby V.O.X: 1997–2006
  • Known for her leadership and strong stage presence.
  • Continued to influence K-pop and inspire future idols after the group's disbandment.

Baby V.O.X History: 2000–2002

The group reaches new heights with hit albums and chart-topping singles!

  • 2000: Release of the album Why with hit singles "Betrayal" and "Coincidence", both reaching #1 in the Korean charts.
  • 2001: Album Boyish Story released, including singles like "Game Over" and "Doll".
  • 2002: The group continues to perform actively, solidifying their popularity across Asia with numerous awards and TV appearances.

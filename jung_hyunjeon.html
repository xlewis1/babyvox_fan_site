*** BABY V.O.X HISTORY TIMELINE * MEMBER PROFILE ARCHIVE: JUNG HYUN JEON (1997 DEBUT MEMBER) ***

Basic Information

  • Name: Jung Hyun Jeon
  • Role in Baby V.O.X: Lead Vocalist
  • Years Active in Group: 1997
  • Status: Original member

Role in the Group

Jung Hyun Jeon served as the original lead vocalist of Baby V.O.X. Her voice helped define the group’s early sound and contributed to their debut album.

As one of the founding members, she played a key role in establishing the group’s musical identity.

Departure from Baby V.O.X

Jung Hyun Jeon left the group on her own terms during the early formation period. Her decision was personal and amicable, allowing the group to continue evolving.

Replacement by Shim Eun Jin

After Jung Hyun Jeon’s departure in 1998, she was replaced by Shim Eun Jin, who took over as lead vocalist and went on to become a mainstay of the group.

Legacy

Although her tenure was brief, Jung Hyun Jeon’s contributions as the original lead vocalist were important to Baby V.O.X’s foundation and early development.

Her role paved the way for the lineup that would later achieve greater fame and long-lasting success.

Kan Mi Youn

*** FAN SHRINE CORNER * STATUS: LEGENDARY MAIN VOCALIST & SOLO ARTRESS * WELCOME TO THE FAN ARCHIVE! ***

Kan Mi Youn is a South Korean singer who gained fame as the main vocalist of the first-generation K-pop girl group Baby V.O.X. After the group disbanded in 2006, she launched a successful solo career and became known for her strong vocals, charismatic stage presence, and appearances on variety shows.

  • Born: February 2, 1983, Seoul, South Korea
  • Baby V.O.X Tenure: 1997–2006, Main Vocalist
  • Solo Debut: 2006 with her album Refreshing, featuring hit singles that charted in Korea
  • Notable Songs: "Remember," "Going Crazy," "Touch," and collaborations with other K-pop artists
  • Variety Shows: Known for appearances on shows like X-Man, Love Letter, and Star Golden Bell, showcasing her humor and personality
  • Other Work: Modeled for commercials, hosted music programs, and remained active in entertainment
  • Legacy: Remembered as one of the strong vocalists of first-generation K-pop and a key member of Baby V.O.X who inspired future girl groups

Kan Mi Youn continues to be celebrated by fans of Baby V.O.X and K-pop enthusiasts for her contribution to the early Hallyu wave.

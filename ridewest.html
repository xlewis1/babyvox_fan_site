Ride West

Release Date: March 18, 2004

Genre: K‑Pop, Dance‑Pop, Hip Hop, Ballad

Bio / Fun Facts:
Ride West is the seventh and final studio album by Baby V.O.X before their official disbandment in 2006.

The album is notable for its blend of English, Korean, Chinese, and Japanese tracks, reflecting a deliberate push toward the global market with influences from hip‑hop and Western pop.

Producers included international collaborators, and the album contains multiple versions of key songs like “Xcstasy” and “Play Remix,” including English and Korean variations.

Though not as commercially successful as some earlier albums, *Ride West* remains special for its ambitious cross‑cultural experimentation and serves as the closing chapter of Baby V.O.X’s original discography.

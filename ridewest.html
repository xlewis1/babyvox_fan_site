Ride West

📅 Release Date: March 18, 2004

🎤 Lineup Line: Kim E‑Z, Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn, Yoon Eun‑hye, Lee Hee‑jin

🎸 Genre: K‑Pop / Dance‑Pop / Hip Hop / Crossover

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Ride West marks the seventh and final studio album delivered by Baby V.O.X before their official transformation and conclusion in 2006. This final release stood out as an ambitious, boundary-pushing effort to integrate into the Western hip-hop market and expand multi-language reach.

The record explores cross-cultural production models, seamlessly containing specific versions sung in English, Korean, Chinese, and Japanese. Main tracks like “Xcstasy” and “Play Remix” championed an aggressive sound shift. While the experimental stylistic pivot challenged conventional mainstream patterns of the time, Ride West remains a daring, historic conclusion to the original discography of first-generation pioneers.

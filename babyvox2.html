Release Date: September 15, 1998

Members during this era:
Lee Gai, Kim E‑Z, Lee Hee‑jin,
Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn

Genre: K‑Pop / Dance‑Pop / Ballad

Bio & Fun Facts:
The second studio album by Baby V.O.X continued the group’s evolution from their debut year. “YaYaYa” served as the title track and became one of their most recognizable early hits. (also a recognizable meme in Kpop culture)

This album showcased the updated lineup after earlier member changes. It includes a mix of dance tracks and vocal songs reflecting the late‑90s K‑Pop style.

"YaYaYa" and "Change" were promoted as singles during this era, helping to raise Baby V.O.X’s profile and popularity in Korea. The album blends upbeat pop with melodic ballads typical of the period.

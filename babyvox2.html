Baby V.O.X II

📅 Release Date: September 15, 1998

🎤 Members during this era: Lee Gai, Kim E‑Z, Lee Hee‑jin, Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn

🎸 Genre: K‑Pop / Dance‑Pop / Ballad

📝 Bio & Fun Facts:
The second studio album by Baby V.O.X continued the group’s evolution from their debut year. “YaYaYa” served as the title track and became one of their most recognizable early hits, remaining a beloved classic in K-pop pop culture history.

This entry is notably the only studio album to feature member Lee Gai. The release showcased an updated visual identity, blending upbeat dance tracks with the smooth, melodic vocal ballads typical of the late‑90s music scene. Both "YaYaYa" and "Change" were heavily promoted, significantly raising Baby V.O.X's profile and commercial status in Korea.

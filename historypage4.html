Baby V.O.X History: 2006–2010

Relive the final years of Baby V.O.X before the reunion period!

  • February 5, 2006: Baby V.O.X officially disbands after nearly 9 years in the industry.
  • 2006–2009: Members focus on individual projects, acting, and solo music careers.
  • 2010: The group reunites for a special appearance on the music talk show "Kim Jung-eun's Chocolate".

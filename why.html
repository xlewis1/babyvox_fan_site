Why

📅 Release Date: May 15, 2000

🎤 Members: Kim E‑Z, Lee Hee‑jin, Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn, Yoon Eun‑hye

🎸 Genre: K‑Pop, R&B, Dance‑Pop

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Why is the fourth full-length studio album by Baby V.O.X, landing in the spring of 2000. This era captured the group operating at their peak commercial momentum, benefiting from pristine vocal arrangements and sophisticated pop instrumentation.

The title track “Why” and the heavy hitting follow-up single “Betrayal” were promoted aggressively across national music charts. To prepare for this era, the five members trained extensively alongside international choreographers and professional vocal engineers, resulting in an exceptionally polished, fiercely mature performance package.

Bio / Fun Facts:
Why is the fourth Korean‑language studio album by Baby V.O.X, released in the spring of 2000. This era saw the group gaining wider commercial success and more polished production than before.

The album features the title track “Why” and the follow‑up single “Betrayal,” both of which were promoted heavily on Korean music shows. The group trained with international choreographers and vocal coaches for this release, blending powerful visuals with a matured sound.

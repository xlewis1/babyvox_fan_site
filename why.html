Genre: K‑Pop, R&B, Dance‑Pop

Bio / Fun Facts:
Why is the fourth Korean‑language studio album by Baby V.O.X, released in the spring of 2000. This era saw the group gaining wider commercial success and more polished production than before.

The album features the title track “Why” and the follow‑up single “Betrayal,” both of which were promoted heavily on Korean music shows during the year. The group trained with international choreographers and vocal coaches for this release, and its concept blended powerful visuals with a matured sound.

During promotions, unusual broadcast regulations in Korea caused performance challenges, but the album still stands as one of the group’s key releases in the early 2000s.

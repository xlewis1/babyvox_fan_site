Bio / Fun Facts:
Equalizeher (also stylized as “Voice of Xpression”) is the debut full‑length album by Baby V.O.X. It was released in early July 1997 with “Haircut” as the final title track.

Originally, the song “To Men (Democracy)” was meant to be the lead track but was banned from broadcast due to controversial lyrics, and was replaced by “Haircut.” During promotions, Cha Yumi was replaced by Kan Miyoun and later Shim Eun Jin joined after further lineup changes.

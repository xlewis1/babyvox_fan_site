Equalizeher

📅 Release Date: July 3, 1997

🎤 Members: Jeong Hyeonjeon, Jung Shiwoon, Cha Yumi, Kim EZ, Lee Heejin

🏢 Label: DR Music / Cream Records

🎸 Genre: Dance, Ballad / 90s K‑Pop

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Equalizeher (also stylized as “Voice of Xpression”) is the debut full-length album by Baby V.O.X. Originally, "To Men (Democracy)" was planned as the lead track but was banned from broadcasting, leading it to be replaced by "Haircut." Cha Yumi was subsequently replaced by Kan Miyoun, and Shim Eun Jin joined shortly after following further lineup changes.

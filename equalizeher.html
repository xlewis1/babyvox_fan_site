Equalizeher

Release Date: July 3, 1997

Members at the time:
Jeong Hyeonjeon, Jung Shiwoon, Cha Yumi,
Kim EZ, Lee Heejin

Label: DR Music / Cream Records

Genre: Dance, Ballad / 90s K‑Pop

Bio / Fun Facts:
Equalizeher (also stylized as “Voice of Xpression”) is the debut full-length album by Baby V.O.X. Originally, "To Men (Democracy)" was planned as lead track but banned from broadcast, replaced by "Haircut." Cha Yumi was replaced by Kan Miyoun; Shim Eun Jin joined after lineup changes.

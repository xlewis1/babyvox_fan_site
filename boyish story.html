Genre: K‑Pop / R&B / Dance‑Pop / Ballad

Bio / Fun Facts:
*Boyish Story* is the fifth Korean‑language studio album by Baby V.O.X, released in 2001 during the group’s evolution toward more self‑produced work. All five members participated creatively on this release, with member contributions to lyrics and composition to diversify the group’s voice and sound.

The title track “Game Over” embraced a more energetic and “boyish” aesthetic and showed the group breaking from the traditional sexy concept toward a confident and bold style. The album includes a notable mix of upbeat tracks and emotional ballads that showcase the group’s range.

Though not as commercially dominant as some earlier releases, *Boyish Story* remains a fan‑loved and creatively ambitious part of Baby V.O.X’s discography.

Special Album

Release Date: April 23, 2002

Members during this era:
Kim E‑Z, Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn,
Yoon Eun‑hye, Lee Hee‑jin

Album Type: Compilation / Special Album

Bio / Fun Facts:
*Special Album* is a major compilation released to celebrate the group’s fifth anniversary and career highlights. The final product included three CDs and one VCD with music video footage and exclusive content.

Originally, the plan was for a five‑disc set (two VCDs, a remix CD, a ballad & hits CD, and an unreleased tracks CD), but this was reduced due to pricing and content limitations to a 3 CD + 1 VCD package.

The album features the World Cup–themed title track *By Chance* (also known as “Coincidence”) and the song “Go.” It became one of the group’s best‑selling releases to date.

🏅 5th Anniversary Golden Era 🏅

Special Album

📅 Release Date: April 23, 2002

🎤 Members: Kim E‑Z, Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn, Yoon Eun‑hye, Lee Hee‑jin

💿 Album Type: Compilation / 5th Anniversary Special Box Set

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Special Album is a massive milestone compilation released to celebrate the group's 5th anniversary and trace their historic career highlights. The physical package includes 3 audio CDs alongside an exclusive VCD containing music video archives and rare behind-the-scenes footage.

Originally envisioned as a premium 5-disc box set (featuring a standalone remix disc, ballad assembly, unreleased session cuts, and two video discs), production caps and consumer retail pricing models streamlined it into the optimized 4-disc package. Driven by the explosive, iconic World Cup promotional title track Coincidence (우연) and the smash follow-up "Go", this became one of Baby V.O.X's most successful and top-selling physical releases.

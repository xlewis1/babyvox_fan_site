Jung Si Woon

Basic Information

Name: Jung Si Woon

Role in Baby V.O.X: Leader, Main Rapper

Years Active in Group: 1997

Status: Original member

Role in the Group

Jung Si Woon was one of the founding members of Baby V.O.X and served as the group’s original leader and main rapper.

In the group’s earliest era, she was responsible for guiding the members, helping establish their direction, and shaping the group’s early image.

Departure from Baby V.O.X

Jung Si Woon later left the group due to personal reasons. Her departure occurred during Baby V.O.X’s early formation period, before the group reached its peak popularity.

Unlike later lineup changes, her exit was not surrounded by controversy and is generally understood as a private decision.

Leadership Handover

Following Jung Si Woon’s departure, the roles of leader and main rapper were handed to Kim EZ.

Kim EZ would go on to hold both positions for the remainder of Baby V.O.X’s career, becoming one of the most defining figures in the group’s history.

Legacy

Jung Si Woon’s time in Baby V.O.X was brief, but her role as the group’s original leader makes her an important part of their foundation.

She represents the earliest chapter of Baby V.O.X — a period of experimentation, change, and transition that ultimately shaped the group’s future success.

Genre: K‑Pop, Eurotrance, R&B, Dance‑Pop

Bio / Fun Facts:
Devotion is the sixth Korean‑language studio album by Baby V.O.X, released in spring 2003. It marked a continued evolution in sound with Eurotrance and modern dance influences under producer Kim Chang‑hwan’s direction.

The album’s lead track “What Should I Do” became one of the group’s standout singles of the era. The album also included multiple styles from emotional ballads to energetic dance numbers, helping expand their popularity across Asia.

*Devotion* sold strongly in several markets and saw Baby V.O.X charting in China, Thailand, and other regions during this era.

Devotion

📅 Release Date: April 3, 2003

🎤 Members: Kim E‑Z, Shim Eun‑jin, Kan Mi‑youn, Yoon Eun‑hye, Lee Hee‑jin

🎸 Genre: K‑Pop / Eurotrance / R&B / Dance‑Pop

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Devotion is the sixth full-length Korean studio album by Baby V.O.X, framing their most experimental and radical genre pivot into commercial Eurotrance, hyper-pop synths, and high-energy club anthems.

The smash lead single “What Should I Do” was aggressively promoted across television, instantly becoming an era-defining track for early-2000s K-pop. Seamlessly blending high-impact dance cuts with sophisticated R&B vocal layers, this project expanded Baby V.O.X's operational footprint completely across international lines, sparking massive chart success and multi-market stadium tours throughout China, Thailand, and across Asia.

✨ Yoon Eun Hye Debut Era ✨

Come Come Come Baby

📅 Release Date: July 21, 1999

🎤 Members: Kim EZ, Lee Hee Jin, Shim Eun Jin, Kan Mi Youn, Yoon Eun Hye

🎸 Genre: K‑Pop / Dance‑Pop / Funk / Ballad

📝 Bio / Fun Facts:
Come Come Come Baby is the third full studio album by Baby V.O.X and marks a historic milestone: the debut of the definitive, classic five‑member lineup following the addition of 15-year-old Yoon Eun Hye. This release guided the group's transition toward an increasingly confident, fiercely mature performance style.

The title track “Get Up” completely swept across mainstream broadcasts, rocketing the group to their very first music show Number 1 placements. Follow-up promotions for the iconic, high-octane track “Killer” and the beautiful ballad “Missing You” further cemented Baby V.O.X as absolute titans of the first-generation K-pop landscape.

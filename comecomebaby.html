Come Come Come Baby

Release Date: July 21, 1999

Members during this era:
Kim EZ, Lee Hee Jin, Shim Eun Jin,
Kan Mi Youn, Yoon Eun Hye

Genre: K‑Pop / Dance‑Pop / Funk / Ballad

Bio / Fun Facts:
Come Come Come Baby is the third full studio album by Baby V.O.X and the first to feature the classic five‑member lineup with Yoon Eun Hye after Lee Gai’s departure. It marked a stylistic shift toward a more confident, mature sound and helped propel the group into greater mainstream success in 1999.

The album’s title track “Get Up” became a major highlight and contributed to the group earning significant music show wins and broader popularity throughout Korea.
